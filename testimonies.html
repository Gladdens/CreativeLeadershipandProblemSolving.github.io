Testimonials

GLADDENS

Student

Growing up, I grappled with school due to a learning disability that went undiagnosed until age five in Hong Kong's educational system, leaving me feeling inadequate and ashamed of my academic struggles. However, a pivotal change came when my family relocated to Canada for a more inclusive education. In this new environment, I discovered diversity and received support tailored to my learning needs, which allowed me to embrace my uniqueness and let go of the pressure to conform to arbitrary standards. With the encouragement of supportive teachers, I learned to appreciate my strengths, particularly as a kinesthetic learner, leading to my success in rhythmic gymnastics, culminating in participation in world championships. Reflecting on my journey, I now recognize that past failures were instrumental in discovering my true capabilities, and I credit my parents' sacrifices for enabling my growth and achievements. Happy FAIL-A-BRATION!

STUDENT 2

Student

I believe Creative Problem Solving methodology is such a great way to solve a problem. I was assigned to a group and was to find a problem to solve. The group agreed with going for a sleep issue with students. Using the methodology, we were able to take step by step process and reach our final goal of getting a good solution to the problem.

During the process, one of the memorable moments I had was when team-mates and I had to bring up the ideas. I believe the ideate stage during the process was the most interesting since I was able to see how others may bring up ideas, and how they thought about the problem. There were challenging moments where we had to start finding realistic solutions to our problems. Since we had to consider the target audience we have chosen for the problem, and how it should fit with many factors, it was difficult to find the best answer from the ideas. But at the end when we finally reached an agreement to a certain idea, it was one of the unforgettable moments.

Furthermore, with the methodology, I felt the importance of cooperation when solving the problem. The methodology is very effective to find a creative problem, but when it comes to a cooperative team, it brings a greater effect to the result. It was hard at moments where we had to find the right time for everyone to join the meeting and discuss the ideas, but finalizing the idea, I have learnt how effective it can be with both the method and a good team.

It was a great decision to use the Creative Problem Solving methodology as a way to solve a problem and I am planning to apply the methodology on whatever I can too.
